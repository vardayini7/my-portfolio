Projects

Enhanced Condensation on Porous Copper Surfaces

The aim of our project was to enhance condensation by modifying the surface design. This project demonstrates the changes in the rate of condensation and heat transfer efficiency caused by altering the surfaces of copper plate and copper foam. The results clearly depict that superhydrophobic copper foam and superhydrophilic copper plate gave the best outcomes. Future work can be to use these surface treatments to enhance the rate of heat transfer in large-scale engineering systems.
